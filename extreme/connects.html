CONNECT

We will strive to create an atmosphere where each student can excel in all areas of Ballroom Dance at all levels. Our main focus is to improve technical skill by teaching proper ballroom technique, and expecting our students and teachers to work hard and be disciplined. The goal for each dance team is to create team unity, learn proper sportsmanship, courtesy, inclusion and responsibility and reach for our highest level of dancing as a formation group. Each student is important to us and will be treated with respect and love. We strive for excellence!

Staci Huston

Staci : Owner of Extreme- Director of Youth & Jr. Gold Teams and Youth Blue, Gold, Jr. Blue & Gold Tech. classes. - Ballroom teacher for over 22 years.
