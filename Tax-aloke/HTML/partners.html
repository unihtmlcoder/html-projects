Audit Assurance relieves tax professionals from the arduous and time consuming task of analyzing IRS correspondence and then re-analyzing clients’ records and documents. All cases are handled in a prompt, courteous and professional manner.

Unique Coverage

Audit Assurance reaches out in ways other audit assistance plans do not. When credits, such as EIC, Child Tax Credit and Education Credit are denied, Protection Plus will assist clients even if the return is yet unfunded by the IRS. In addition, the Protection Plus Audit Assistance plan includes Schedule A, Schedule C and Schedule E for example, at no additional charge.

Tax Preparation Guarantee

The taxpayer can rest easy knowing participating tax preparers’ services are guaranteed. If a legitimate preparer error is made during the filing of a tax return, the taxpayer will be reimbursed for the consequent amounts due to the IRS up to the first $2500*.

Tax Debt Relief

Audit Assurance offers help to taxpayers who owe debts to the IRS. When additional taxes, penalties, and interest are assessed, working with the IRS can be a confusing and stressful experience. Our staff will work with the taxpayer for a resolution to their debt. Tax Debt Relief includes, but is not limited to:

  • Guaranteed Installment Agreements
  • Wage Garnishment Relief
  • Offer in Compromise
  • Tax Penalty Abatement
  • Streamline Installment Agreements
  • Tax Liens
  • Innocent Spouse Relief
  • Rejected ITIN (W-7) Applications
