AUDIT PROTECTION PLAN

The Internal Revenue Service and the State taxing authorities have significantly increased their audit activity in recent years and periodically announce new programs for auditing more taxpayers.

For our clients, this often means substantial fees when we are asked to handle audits or resolve questions raised by the IRS or State tax agencies We offer a comprehensive Audit Protection Plan (APP) designed to eliminate your worry about additional fees you would otherwise be responsible for while being represented during an audit.Your audit protection plan includes the following services.

  • All correspondence to resolve letters of inquiry relating to your Federal and State business and personal income tax returns and state sales and use tax returns.
  • Any audit of your Federal or State business and personal income tax returns, state sales and use tax returns and Unemployment audits up to the agent level
  • Any audit notices or letter of inquiry from the Federal or State government relating to your business and personal tax returns and state sales and use tax returns.
